Red Black

Release Year: 2025

Description: An eclectic grouping of songs that reflect a darker tone embodying one of Wild's many perspectives on life, love and the pursuit of such things. Transcending genre, he seeks to communicate in ways that aren't usually expressed and are often considered detractions from the usual discographic structure of an artists portrayal of self.

Black Blue

Release Year: 2025

Description: An equal antithesis to Red Rum, Wild establishes an equally dark tone that communicates a sense of progression towards the intended upward momentum that counters the low-lighting edge of its counterpart. Blue Black symbolizes hope in a dark world that one experiences from a position of loneliness and regular antagonism.
